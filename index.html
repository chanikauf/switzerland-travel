Why Arosa?

Arosa, the ultimate Winter escape.

Nestled in a picturesque valley, Arosa stands out as the best place to visit in the winter due to its breathtaking landscapes, diverse winter sports, and charming alpine culture. This idyllic Swiss resort town boasts pristine ski slopes suitable for all skill levels, from beginners to seasoned professionals, ensuring an exhilarating experience for every winter sports enthusiast.
Beyond skiing, visitors can indulge in activities like snowshoeing, ice skating on the frozen Obersee Lake, or exploring the enchanting winter hiking trails that offer panoramic views of the surrounding peaks. With its cozy chalets, welcoming locals, and a range of delightful culinary experiences, Arosa captures the essence of a perfect winter retreat, promising unforgettable memories for all who visit.
